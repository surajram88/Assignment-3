About Waverley Exhaust and Brake

Waverley Exhaust & Brake Centre have been servicing Melbourne’s south eastern suburbs for over 25 years.

We are not your traditional auto repairer …

We specialise in helping you find the right service and maintenance program for your family or work situation.

Our services include all mechanical repairs, brakes, clutches, state-of-the-art custom and replacement exhaust systems and log-book servicing. We are a registered VACC A-Grade repairer and also issue Roadworthy Certificates (RWC) as a VicRoads Licensed Vehicle Tester.

We offer while-you-wait service and/or drop-off in the local area.
